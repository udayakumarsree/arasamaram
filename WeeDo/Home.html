Air Filter Change

A 1" pleated air filter must be replaced every 30-60 days. A 2" pleated air filter should be swapped out every 3 months. A 3” pleated air filter should be changed out every 120 days.

Do you really know when to change? You can check with us.

Battery-check

Do you experience ​dim headlights, slow engine crank, clicking noise when starting the car, the engine backfires, smell of sulfur, swollen battery case, corrosion of the battery terminals, etc.?
